Amidst rising sea levels, a treacherous 9-headed hydra has appeared in the Arctic. Except this monster seems incredibly... bored? Reports are coming in that its yawns of disinterest can be heard from miles away. Locals are said to be calling it the Apathydra.

Heroes, to accomplish the herculean task of slaying this beast, you will need to sever all 9 of the Apathydra's heads at once. To do this, solve the 9 minipuzzles, determine which weapon is best suited for which head, and ultimately reveal what will deliver the final blow.

The answer to each of the 9 Head minipuzzles is a single word. You can use the answer checker to confirm these intermediate answers prior to submitting the final answer.

This code sheet may come in handy for some of the minipuzzles.
