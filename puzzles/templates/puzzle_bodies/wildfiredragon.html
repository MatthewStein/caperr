Raging brush fires have been appearing all around the world with increasing frequency and severity. Empowered by the flames, an enigmatic Wild Fire Dragon has emerged after centuries dormant, leaving us with more questions than answers.

But where better to find answers than on a classic quiz show!

Once you've determined the answers, you may need to look at how they start to figure out another way to solve the quiz. Only then can you bring in the money and reveal the Wild Fire Dragon's secret defense.
