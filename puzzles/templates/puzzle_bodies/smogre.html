In cities all around the world, a Smogre has been sighted around various tall structures. Much more malicious than its Shrek-like cousins, the Smogre feeds off and disguises itself in polluted air.

We've received a dozen reports of Smogre sightings. Each report noted the structure where the Smogre was initially sighted, the route followed while tracking the creature, and the final location where the Smogre was sighted in that city before vanishing in a cloud of smog. Just one small problem: the directions got all jumbled in our system, and we aren't sure which direction goes with which structure and final sighting image.

The Smogre seemed intent on concealing certain data at each of its final sighting locations. Perhaps if you can recover that data, you can determine what can be used to trap the Smogre once and for all.
