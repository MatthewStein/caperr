Hints

Puzzle #1: Trash Monster

Getting started #1

Start by solving the clues in the Recycled Items Output section. The numbers in parentheses indicate the lengths of each answers, and the clues are listed alphabetically by answer.

Getting started #2

Work backwards from the Output answers, trying to reverse the transformations into objects found in the Trash Monster. Transformations 1 and 3 are simpler ones to get started with.

Using the transformations

Examples of each transformation: (1) PRESENT -> SERPENT, (2) TRAIN -> IRRITANT, (3) CHAIR -> CHAI, (4) CARROT -> CAR + ROT, (5) GRILL -> GUERILLA

I've found all the items... now what?

Once you’ve identified all the recycled items, pay attention to where they’re located in the Trash Monster.


Puzzle #2: The Apathydra

Getting started

Start by solving the 9 Head minipuzzles. Once you have enough minipuzzle answers, you'll be able to figure out what to do with the weapons.

Head 1

5 words are missing a letter. You can determine what the words should actually be by context.

Head 2

A letter in the NATO phonetic alphabet is "hidden" in each phrase, spanning multiple words.

Head 3

This is a Sudoku with letters. Even though it's sized a bit differently than a normal 9x9 Sudoku, the same rules still apply.

Head 4

The 5 all-caps words are guesses in a game of Wordle.

Head 5

The animals you seek are: BEETLE, CAMEL, CROCODILE, DEER, HAMSTER, ROOSTER, SHREW, SNAKE, TOAD, and TURTLE.

Head 6

The items in the left column are: CUPID, DIAMOND, SOPRANO, OGRE, and DOC.

The items in the right column are: GRUMPY, BASS, HEART, VIXEN, and NIGHTCRAWLER.

Head 7

The answers to these 4 clues will form a word square.

Head 8

For each 2x3 grid, the items that are not actually in the listed category form letters in Braille.

Head 9

Upon solving the Hashi puzzle, the double-bridged edges will draw out letters.

Weapons: What are the numbers?

The number attached to each weapon is an index. (For example, if you had the index 3 with the word EXAMPLE, you'd extract the third letter, A.)

Weapons: What are the weapons?

From left to right: BOMB, BOW, CLUB, FIRE, HAMMER, SPEAR, STICK, SWORD, and WHIP.

Weapons: The final answer

After pairing weapons with Head answers, use the weapon numbers to index into the corresponding Head answers (in Head order) to get the final 2-word answer.


Puzzle #3: The Wild Fire Dragon

Getting started

Start off by solving the trivia questions. Each question has exactly one correct answer from amongst the four listed options. Internet research is absolutely allowed.

I've solved the questions. What now?

As instructed, "you may need to look at how they start to figure out another way to solve the quiz" — pay attention to the first letters of the correct answers.

I thought I solved it, but there's more?

If you've discovered the name of a song, you are halfway there! Look up the lyrics to that song and solve the quiz again with all new answers.

How do I use the prices?

Use the dollar amounts as indices into the second layer answers, i.e. if you had $5 and the answer ANSWER, you'd take the fifth letter = E. Ignore spaces and punctuation when counting.
