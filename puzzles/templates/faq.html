FAQ

What is this?

A puzzle hunt for the ESG team at PwC.

What is a puzzle hunt?

A puzzle game where puzzles can take many different forms and each puzzle’s answer is an English word or phrase. Puzzles typically have no direct instructions, so it’s up to you to discover patterns and figure out how to make sense of the information you’re given.

If you're new to puzzle hunts and want to see some examples, or if you’re looking for some extra practice, we recommend checking out some other online puzzle hunts such as Colby’s Curious Cookoff and Puzzled Pint.

How many puzzles will there be?

Five. Four main puzzles and one final metapuzzle.

When is the game?

The first puzzle will be released at noon EST on Wednesday, October 12, 2022. New puzzles will be released weekly on Wednesdays through November 9.

Are there prizes?

Yes! There will be a random prize drawing amongst the teams that have solved each week's puzzle. Teams have until the next week's puzzle is posted to submit their answer.

Can I use the internet?

Yes! Online research is allowed and in some cases required to solve these puzzles.

I’m stuck on a puzzle. What should I do?

Here are some general puzzle tips that might be useful:

  • Thoroughly check the work that you’ve already done. Fixing a small mistake or incorrect assumption can greatly help with getting unstuck.
  • Get fresh eyes on the puzzle.
  • Take a break and come back to it.
  • Consider what information you haven’t used yet.
  • It can be useful to put everything you have into your favorite search engine.
